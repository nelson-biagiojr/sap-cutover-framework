# AI for SAP Cutover Management

## Objective

Leverage AI to enhance decision-making and risk anticipation during cutover.

## Use Cases

### 1. Risk Identification

Prompt:
"Analyze this cutover plan and identify potential risks and blind spots."

### 2. Executive Reporting

Prompt:
"Generate a concise executive summary based on the current incident list."

### 3. Decision Support

Prompt:
"What is the most critical risk if this activity fails during cutover?"

## Benefits

- Faster decision-making
- Improved risk visibility
- Better communication quality

## Positioning

Combines traditional SAP delivery expertise with AI-enabled management.
